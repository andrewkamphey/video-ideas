# Examples

Short worked sessions showing facilitator tone. Read only when stuck on how a tool should behave.

---

## Beginner (List mode)

**Input:** "Freelance bookkeeper, 8 years, QuickBooks + small biz taxes."

**Facilitator:** "Dumping beginner questions — no answers from me unless you ask."

**Output (fragment):**
- What is bookkeeping vs accounting?
- Do I need QuickBooks or can I use a spreadsheet?
- What receipts do I actually need to save?
- When do I hire a bookkeeper vs do it myself?
- What's a chart of accounts?
- How far back can I catch up messy books?
- What does "reconciling" mean in plain English?

## Why (interactive)

**Input:** Concept: "Why teach people to negotiate salary."

**Facilitator moves:**
1. Why teach salary negotiation? → User: "Most people leave money on the table."
2. Why do they leave it? → User: "They don't know their market rate."
3. Why don't they know? → … (continues to depth 5)

**Output:** Sharpened spark — "The number you're afraid to Google is the number you should film about."

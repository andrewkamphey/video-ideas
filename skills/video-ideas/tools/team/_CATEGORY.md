# Team category

Multiplayer facilitation for workshops, co-hosts, and group brainstorms. **No implemented tools in v1** — this stub defines norms and future tool names.

## Facilitation norms

- One facilitator voice — you hold the rail; humans supply expertise.
- Round-robin or popcorn: one person answers per step unless the tool says otherwise.
- Same **start, don't complete** rule: sparks and titles, not finished calendars.
- Capture attribution lightly ("Alex: …", "Sam: …") when multiple voices contribute.
- Defer to the expert in the room when domain knowledge conflicts.
